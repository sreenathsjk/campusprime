Our Story

About CampusPrime

We're on a mission to modernize school management in India, one school at a time.

Who We Are

CampusPrime was founded in 2023 with a single goal: to give every Indian school — from a 50-student rural school to a 5,000-student urban institution — access to world-class management tools.

We're a team of educators, engineers, and entrepreneurs who have seen firsthand the pain of managing school operations with spreadsheets, paper registers, and disconnected software. We built CampusPrime to solve that comprehensively.

Our Mission

To empower every school in India with affordable, reliable, and easy-to-use software that saves time, reduces errors, and improves education quality through better administration.

Our Vision

A future where every school administrator in India can focus on what truly matters — student learning — because CampusPrime handles the operational burden.

Why CampusPrime?

  • Made for India: INR pricing, GST-ready receipts, Indian academic calendar, and regional language readiness.
  • No IT team needed: Cloud-based, zero-installation, works on any smartphone or computer.
  • Affordable: Starting at ₹15,000/year — less than ₹1,250/month for complete school management.
  • Secure: Firebase-powered with enterprise-grade security. Your school's data belongs only to you.
  • Reliable: 99.9% uptime SLA backed by Google Cloud infrastructure.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CampusPrime Technologies Pvt. Ltd. collects information you provide when registering your school, including school name, principal name, email address, phone number, and address. We also collect usage data about how you interact with the platform.

1.1 School & User Data

Data entered into CampusPrime — including student records, attendance, fee records, and exam results — belongs entirely to your school. We do not sell, share, or use this data for any purpose other than providing the service.

1.2 Technical Data

We collect browser type, device information, IP address, and usage logs to improve performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the CampusPrime service
  • To send important service notifications and updates
  • To process payments and generate GST invoices
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations under Indian law

3. Data Security

CampusPrime uses Google Firebase (ISO 27001 certified) for data storage and authentication. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Each school's data is completely isolated from other schools.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon termination, you may request complete data deletion within 30 days. A full data export is available at any time from your account settings.

5. Refund Policy

Annual subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of payment if you are not satisfied. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable. To request a refund, email [email protected] with your school name and transaction ID.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management only. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

7. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact our Data Protection Officer at [email protected] or write to: CampusPrime Technologies Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore, Karnataka — 560001, India.
