Capabilities

Everything a school needs, nothing it doesn't

Built for real schools. Designed by educators. Engineered for scale.

👨‍🎓

Student Management

Complete student lifecycle from enrollment to graduation. Manage profiles, promotions, class assignments, and academic history effortlessly.

👨‍🏫

Teacher Management

Onboard teachers, assign subjects and classes, track performance, and manage payroll-ready records all in one place.

📅

Attendance System

Mark daily attendance for students and staff. Get real-time reports, trend analysis, and automated parent notifications for absences.

💳

Fee Management

Create custom fee structures, record payments, generate professional receipts, and track outstanding dues with automated reminders.

📝

Exams & Results

Schedule exams, enter marks, auto-calculate grades, publish results, and generate downloadable report cards in seconds.

📢

Communication Hub

Send targeted announcements to students, teachers, or parents. Email and SMS-ready integrations keep everyone in the loop.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time insights on enrollment trends, attendance patterns, fee collection rates, and academic performance — all visualized beautifully.

🔒

Multi-Tenant Security

Each school's data is completely isolated. Role-based access, JWT auth, rate limiting, and enterprise-grade security built in from day one.

☁️

Cloud-Native & Scalable

Built on Next.js, PostgreSQL, and containerized with Docker. Scales from 50 to 50,000 students without breaking a sweat.
